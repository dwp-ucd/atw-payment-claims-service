Claiming from your grant

If you’re awarded an Access to Work grant, you can use the money to buy the support you need. You or your employer might need to pay for your support first, then claim the money back from your Access to Work grant. You can make a claim online or by post.

If you need the form in other formats, such as braille, large print or audio CD, call the Access to Work helpline.

Make a claim online

Make a claim by post

The quickest way to make a claim is online. If you cannot claim online, you can send Access to Works claims by post.

You’ll need to:

  • log in to your Access to Work account to download a claim form
  • print off and fill in the claim form
  • include any receipts or invoices we ask for in the claim form

You can call the Access to Work helpline and ask for a claim form to be sent to you if you cannot print it off.

Send your claim to access to work
